Privacy Preservation:

We are committed to protecting user privacy and being transparent about our data practices. Our application shares user information with third parties only when it is necessary for the operation of the app, with explicit user consent, or in accordance with legal requirements.

Our app collects various types of information, including but not limited to device identifiers such as Advertising ID, Android ID, IMEI, BSSID, and MAC address. This collection helps improve app functionality, provide personalized content, and support advertising and analytics.

The app is committed to respecting human culture and rich history without distorting facts.

Secure Use of Information:

The app is committed to not using the information within it to incite terrorist acts, spread extremist ideas, or promote harmful societal issues.

Provision of Health and Educational Facilities Information:

The app is committed to providing information on health, educational, and service facilities that may benefit users.

Continuous Correction and Update of Information:

In case any errors or misinformation are discovered, the app commits to immediately correcting them and regularly updating the content to ensure information accuracy.

Support for the App:

The app offers free content, but users can provide optional financial support to help develop and improve the service.

Data Storage:

User data is stored securely and protected, with appropriate measures taken to prevent unauthorized access, data loss, or damage.

Users have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of their personal data from the app's database at any time.
